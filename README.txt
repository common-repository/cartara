This Cartara eCommerce plugin allows users to automatically synchronize their Cartara small business sales automation account products to their WordPress database. 

== Description ==

Cartara.pro is your "instant eCommerce" solution that converts your Wordpress site into a powerful selling machine.

Easily showcase your Cartara products on any page, or location, of your site by copying and pasting a simple short code [cartara_products_showcase] onto the page.

1 plugin… copy & paste… and you're instantly in business!


Cartara.pro is the most powerful all-in-one eCommerce solution and includes the following:

* Automatically updates all products in your WordPress site.
* Powerful up-sell functionality built-in.
* Point-&-Click product management simplicity.
* Integrates videos (and images) for each product to boost conversions.
* Credit card & order page security handled by Cartara 
* Sophisticated product "preview" features your customers expect.
* Unlimited Membership sites included.
* …plus more

Your WordPress site brings the traffic, the Cartara plugin converts that traffic into eCommerce gold.
